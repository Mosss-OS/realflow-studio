# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

✅ **Be Respectful and Inclusive**
- Treat everyone with respect and kindness
- Welcome newcomers and help them learn
- Use inclusive language and avoid stereotypes
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences

✅ **Be Collaborative**
- Work together to solve problems
- Share knowledge and help others learn
- Give and receive constructive feedback
- Build on each other's ideas

✅ **Be Professional**
- Maintain professional conduct in all interactions
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Be open to learning and improvement
- Take responsibility for your actions

✅ **Be Ethical**
- Respect privacy and confidentiality
- Give proper credit for others' work
- Follow intellectual property laws
- Be honest and transparent

### Unacceptable Behavior

❌ **Harassment**
- Unwelcome comments about appearance, identity, or characteristics
- Threats, intimidation, or bullying
- Unwanted sexual attention or advances
- Stalking or following

❌ **Discrimination**
- Discriminatory jokes or language
- Exclusion based on identity or background
- Microaggressions or subtle discrimination
- Privilege checking or shaming

❌ **Disruption**
- Spamming or off-topic content
- Trolling or deliberate provocation
- Publishing private information
- Sabotaging others' work

❌ **Unethical Conduct**
- Plagiarism or intellectual property theft
- Misrepresentation of credentials or work
- Exploiting community resources
- Conflicts of interest not disclosed

## 📋 Reporting Guidelines

### How to Report
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Direct Contact**: Reach out to the person directly if comfortable
2. **Community Leaders**: Contact community moderators or maintainers
3. **Formal Report**: Submit a formal report to conduct@realflow.studio
4. **Anonymous Report**: Use anonymous reporting channels when available

### What to Include
- **Description**: Clear description of the incident
- **People Involved**: Who was involved (if known)
- **Time and Place**: When and where it happened
- **Impact**: How it affected you or others
- **Evidence**: Any screenshots, logs, or other evidence

### Response Process
1. **Acknowledgment**: We'll acknowledge your report within 24 hours
2. **Investigation**: We'll investigate the incident thoroughly
3. **Action**: We'll take appropriate action based on findings
4. **Follow-up**: We'll follow up with you about the outcome
5. **Prevention**: We'll take steps to prevent future incidents

## 🔧 Enforcement

### Consequences
Unacceptable behavior may result in:

- **Warning**: Private warning about behavior
- **Temporary Suspension**: Temporary removal from community
- **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from community
- **Legal Action**: Involvement of law enforcement if needed
- **Public Disclosure**: Public statement about serious violations

### Appeals Process
If you believe enforcement action was unfair:

1. **Written Appeal**: Submit written appeal to conduct@realflow.studio
2. **Review**: Different leaders will review the appeal
3. **Decision**: Final decision will be communicated
4. **Resolution**: Appropriate resolution will be implemented
